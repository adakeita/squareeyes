Frequently Asked Questions

What is SquareEyes? +

SquareEyes is a movie rental, purchase and theatre all in one! We pride ourself on sharing all our proceeds with the creators so that you can have easy access to quality indie films with the click of a button.

How does it work? +

When you buy a movie from us 60% of the proceed goes directly to the creater of the movie. That way you get to give concrete support to the people who make the art you consume!

What if I don't like the movie? +

As long as you have watched less then half of the movie, you can request a full refund.
