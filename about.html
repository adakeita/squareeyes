Our Story

SquareEyes was founded by film enthusiasts frustrated by the limited options for discovering and supporting independent filmmakers. Our platform connects content watchers with small filmmakers creating incredible stories that often go unrecognized. We allow audiences from all over the world to discover and purchase high-quality videos directly from the creators. Our mission is to be the go-to destination for film lovers who want to support independent creators and discover exciting new stories.

Our Team

At SquareEyes, our team is dedicated to supporting independent filmmakers to ensure their work is showcased well and bringing their work to a suitable audience. With a diverse range of backgrounds and experiences, we bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every aspect of our platform.
From our founders to our developers and customer service representatives, every member of our team shares a love for cinema and a commitment to connecting content watchers with the best films.

But we are more than our employed team, and we stay committed to fostering a good relationship with our community, you can read more about that here.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SquareEyes?

SquareEyes is the movie vendor for the total cinema experience with the click of a button! We pride ourselves on sharing all our proceeds with the creators so that you can have easy access to quality indie films with the click of a button.

How does it work?

When you buy a movie from us 60% of the proceeds go directly to the creator of the movie. That way you get to give concrete support to the people who make the art you consume!

Are there refunds?

We strive for satisfaction on every purchase, but just in case. As long as you have watched less than half of the movie, you can request a full refund.
